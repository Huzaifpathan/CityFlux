# My Bookings Tab - Complete Features List

## 📱 **Tab Structure**

### **Main Sections:**
1. **Active Bookings** (Currently running)
2. **Upcoming Bookings** (Future bookings) 
3. **Past Bookings** (Completed history)
4. **All Bookings** (Combined view)

---

## ✨ **Core Features**

### 🔥 **Active Bookings Section**
- ✅ **Real-time countdown timer** (Time remaining)
- ✅ **Live status tracking** (Confirmed → Active → Ending Soon)
- ✅ **QR code display** (For entry/exit)
- ✅ **Extend booking** button
- ✅ **Get directions** to parking
- ✅ **Call parking support**
- ✅ **Current parking details** (Zone, slot number)
- ✅ **Auto refresh** every 30 seconds

### 📅 **Upcoming Bookings**
- ✅ **Pre-booked slots** for future dates
- ✅ **Reminder settings** (30 min, 1 hour before)
- ✅ **Modify booking** (time, duration, vehicle)
- ✅ **Cancel booking** (with refund policy)
- ✅ **Set parking alarm** 
- ✅ **Add to calendar** integration

### 📜 **Past Bookings History**
- ✅ **Complete booking history** (last 6 months)
- ✅ **Download receipts** (PDF invoices)
- ✅ **Rate & review** parking experience
- ✅ **Rebook same slot** (quick rebook)
- ✅ **View photos** (if uploaded during parking)
- ✅ **Export to Excel/CSV**
